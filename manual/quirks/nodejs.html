NodeJS

eslint-plugin-prettier

When working with NodeJS, everything works as expected, but some projects have settings that can fool nvf.

If https://github.com/prettier/eslint-plugin-prettier">this plugin or similar is included, you might get a situation where your eslint configuration diagnoses your formatting according to its own config (usually .eslintrc.js).

The issue there is your formatting is made via prettierd.

This results in auto-formatting relying on your prettier config, while your eslint config diagnoses formatting https://prettier.io/docs/en/comparison.html">which it's not supposed to)

In the end, you get discrepancies between what your editor does and what it wants.

Solutions are:

  1. Don't add a formatting config to eslint, and separate prettier and eslint.
  2. PR this repo to add an ESLint formatter and configure nvf to use it.
